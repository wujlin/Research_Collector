## Abstract

We propose a Multimodal Iterative Reasoning Molecular Generation framework that integrates molecular graph topology, SMILES sequences, and physicochemical descriptors through a unified multimodal encoder.Our iterative reasoning decoder progressively refines molecular structures through geometric-semantic alternating denoising diffusion across multiple reasoning rounds.A contribution-aware dynamic modality balancing strategy adaptively adjusts the influence of different modalities during generation, ensuring critical structural features are preserved.Experiments on QM9, ZINC250K, and MOSES demonstrate that our method achieves 98.7% validity, 96.1% uniqueness, 94.8% novelty, and 79.5% property hit rate, consistently outperforming state-ofthe-art baselines.
