## Abstract

Concordia is a framework for generative agent-based models in which LLM agents act in physical, social, or digital spaces. Agents combine LLM calls with associative memory retrieval, while a Game Master manages the environment and translates natural-language actions into grounded consequences.

## Why It Matters

This is the most directly relevant Leibo paper for generative social simulation. It moves from MARL benchmark environments to open-ended LLM-based populations.

## Connection

For synthetic cities, Concordia is a conceptual template for simulated residents whose actions are grounded in spatial/social constraints rather than free-form chat alone.
