## Abstract

Melting Pot is a multi-agent RL evaluation suite designed to test generalization to new social partners and situations. It includes many test scenarios covering social dilemmas, reciprocity, resource sharing, and task partitioning.

## Why It Matters

This paper shifts the evaluation question from single-agent score to population-level robustness: can agents behave well with unfamiliar partners under new social conditions?

## Connection

This is a benchmark analogue for synthetic-city agents: evaluate not only distributional fit, but social generalization under changed partner/environment populations.
